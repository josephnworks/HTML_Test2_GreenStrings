Oversharp

Website: www.weareoversharp.com

Oversharp is a pop punk band formed in 2017 and based in Chand, Selwyn. Known for their punchy guitars, breakneck tempos, and lyrics that blend sharp sarcasm with sincere emotion, Oversharp has built a loyal following on the strength of their high-octane sound and no-filter attitude.

Their upcoming album, Blitz, promises a tighter, louder, and even more unrelenting take on their signature style—an explosive mix of rebellion, nostalgia, and raw honesty. From skatepark singalongs to midnight breakdowns, Oversharp continues to channel the energy of growing up loud and feeling everything at once.
