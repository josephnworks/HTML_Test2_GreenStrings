Green Waves

Website: www.greenwavesband.com

Green Waves is a glam rock band that crashes onto the scene with glitter-soaked swagger and a sound that's equal parts thunder and theater. Known for their explosive riffs, soaring vocals, and flamboyant style, Green Waves channels the spirit of '70s glam with a modern pulse—where glam doesn't just shimmer, it roars. Their performances are pure spectacle: neon lights, wild costumes, and an unapologetic celebration of excess and emotion. Beneath the glam, though, are sharp songwriting chops and a raw, beating heart. Green Waves isn't just reviving glam rock—they're reinventing it for a new generation of dreamers, rebels, and misfits.
