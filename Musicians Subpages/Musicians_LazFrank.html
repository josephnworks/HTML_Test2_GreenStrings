Laz Frank

Website: www.lazfrank.com

Lazarus Franklin DeHavilland, professionally known as Laz Frank, is a soft rock singer whose music feels like a late-night drive down an empty highway — introspective, smooth, and quietly powerful. Known for his warm, smoky vocals and timeless melodies, Laz weaves stories of love, longing, and quiet redemption with a gentle touch and poetic soul. His sound blends vintage influences with modern sensibility, echoing the calm intensity of artists like Jackson Browne and John Mayer. With each song, Laz Frank invites listeners into a world where emotion takes the wheel and every lyric feels like a secret shared under fading stars.
