Arkanyu

Website: www.arkanyuband.net

Arkanyu is a high-energy pop rock band hailing from Takeketonga, Selwyn, Blossomland, blending anthemic hooks with cinematic soundscapes. Formed in 2020 by childhood friends Kai Morran (vocals/guitar) and Julian Ardent (drums), with addition of Chad Elfred (bass), Archer Paxton (keys) and Cairo Bahkuizen (percussion/vocals), Arkanyu quickly carved out a loyal following with their emotionally charged lyrics and explosive live performances. Their sound fuses the shimmer of 2000s-era pop punk with the atmospheric depth of modern alt-rock — think Paramore meets M83 on a stormy night. With their debut EP Ghostlight Drive earning underground acclaim, Arkanyu is now poised to break out nationally, channeling Selwyn's haunting beauty into songs that feel both intimate and arena-ready.
