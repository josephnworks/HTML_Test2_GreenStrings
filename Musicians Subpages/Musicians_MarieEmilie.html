Marie Emilie

Website: www.marieemilie.com

Marie Emilie Hoch, known professionally as Marie Emilie, is a heartfelt Selwyn-born singer-songwriter whose music reflects her connection to place and self. Through earnest lyrics and warm melodies, she's crafting an authentic folk-pop and folk rock presence, resonating locally and gradually reaching new listeners beyond her homeland. She was also the first musician signed to the independent record label, Green Strings, which was formed in mid 2024.

Her vocals convey clarity, sincerity, and emotional depth — a storytelling style that invites listeners into her personal reflections and experiences. Often rooted in everyday scenes, emotional honesty, and the beauty of rural life—likely inspired by the Selwyn region's peaceful yet vivid environment.

With local shows and digital presence, she's gathering a loyal following and may soon emerge onto broader national or even international folk-pop stages.
