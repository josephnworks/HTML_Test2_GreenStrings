Firstblood

Website: www.firstbloodband.com

Firstblood is an alternative rock band in Pitau, Selwyn. Their music is a collision of brooding melodies, jagged guitar lines, and lyrics that bite like winter wind. Fueled by restless energy and small-town claustrophobia, Firstblood doesn't just play songs—they carve them out of silence and leave them bleeding on stage.

Their sound walks a tightrope between beauty and violence: reverb-heavy riffs crash against minimalist synths, while thunderous drums echo like something coming through the walls. Known for unpredictable live sets "half ritual and half release", Firstblood has become a cult fixture in the underground circuit, their name whispered like a warning wherever heartbreak festers and hope flickers. In Selwyn, they're not just a band, they're a storm system.
