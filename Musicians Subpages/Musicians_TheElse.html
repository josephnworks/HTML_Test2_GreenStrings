The Else

Website: www.theelseband.com

The Else is a boundary-pushing rock band that thrives in the space between chaos and control. With thunderous riffs, haunting melodies, and a flair for the cinematic, their sound is equal parts grit and grandeur. Drawing from alt-rock, post-punk, and a touch of psychedelic edge, The Else creates music that feels both familiar and eerily new — like echoes from a future you've already lived. Known for their explosive live shows and enigmatic presence, the band doesn't chase trends; they forge their own path, inviting listeners into a dark, electrifying universe where nothing is quite what it seems.

Members:

  • Dakota Weeks - lead vocals
  • Shawn Gardener - guitar
  • Greg Peck - bass
  • Sebastian Fulton - drums, keyboards
