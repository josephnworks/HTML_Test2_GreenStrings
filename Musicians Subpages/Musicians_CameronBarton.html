Cameron Barton

Website: www.cameronbartonmusic.com

Cameron Barton is a rising force in the country rock scene, known for his gravel-road vocals, heartfelt lyrics, and electric stage presence. Hailing from Patiki, Selwyn, Barton blends southern grit with rock 'n' roll edge, drawing inspiration from legends like Tom Petty, Chris Stapleton, and Steve Earle. After years of playing dusty barrooms and opening for bigger names on the festival circuit, he's finally stepping into the spotlight.

His highly anticipated debut album, Walking To Heaven, drops September 4. The record promises a raw, emotional journey through love, loss, redemption, and the long miles in between — anchored by anthemic guitar riffs and Barton's signature raspy soul. With singles like “Dust in My Veins” and “Call It Home” already stirring buzz, Walking To Heaven is poised to put Cameron Barton on the map as one of the most compelling new voices in modern Americana.
