Janine Black

Website: www.janineblack.net

Janine Leona Black is a powerhouse pop rock singer whose voice hits like thunder and lingers like smoke. With a sound that fuses anthemic choruses, gritty guitars, and raw, confessional lyrics, she brings a fierce edge to modern pop. Raised on classic rock records and '90s alt radio, Janine channels rebellion and resilience into every track, delivering performances that are as emotionally intense as they are sonically explosive. Whether she's belting out a heartbreak anthem or a fiery call to arms, Janine Black is a voice for anyone who's ever felt too loud, too honest, or too wild to fit in.
