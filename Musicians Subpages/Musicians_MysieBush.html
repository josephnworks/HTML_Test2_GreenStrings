Mysie Bush

Website: www.mysiebush.com

Zella Mysie Bush is a soft rock singer-songwriter whose music feels like a quiet conversation at golden hour—intimate, reflective, and beautifully unhurried. With a voice that carries both warmth and wistfulness, she writes songs that gently unravel the complexities of love, memory, and finding peace in the in-between moments. Drawing inspiration from folk traditions, vintage rock, and a touch of jazz sensibility, Mysie's sound is timeless yet deeply personal. Her lyrics read like journal entries set to melody, and her performances invite you in, not with spectacle, but with sincerity. In a loud world, Mysie Bush is a soft but unforgettable echo.
