FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions



What is Green Strings?

Green Strings is an independent music record label dedicated to discovering, producing, and promoting emerging and established artists across a variety of rock genres. We value authentic artistry, creative freedom, and sustainable practices in the music industry.



How can I submit my music to Green Strings?

We accept music submissions via our official website. Please visit the "Careers" page and follow the guidelines carefully. Be sure to include a streaming link, a short bio, and any relevant social media or performance history.



Does Green Strings offer artist development or management?

Yes, in addition to recording and distribution, we offer artist development services such as branding, social media strategy, and performance coaching. While we are not a management company, we often collaborate closely with managers and booking agents.



What genres does Green Strings specialize in?

We primarily focus on a variety of rock genres, including pop, alternative, emo, grunge, metal and hardcore, but we’re always open to unique voices and cross-genre experimentation that align with our creative values.



Is Green Strings environmentally focused?

Absolutely. Sustainability is part of our mission. We aim to reduce our carbon footprint through eco-friendly merchandise, digital-first distribution, and green touring initiatives with our artists whenever possible.



Where is Green Strings based?

We are headquartered in Selwyn, Blossomland, but we work with artists from both local and around the world. Much of our collaboration and production can be done remotely or in partner studios.



How can I stay updated on Green Strings artists and releases?

You can follow us on social media (@greenstringslabel), subscribe to our newsletter on our website, or stream our curated playlists on Spotify, Apple Music, and other platforms to stay in the loop with the latest music and artist news.
