Who Are We?

Green Strings is an indepedent record label based in Selwyn. Founded in 2024, we've deliver the purest rock music that anyone can enjoy at any time, ranging from alternative to pop, emo to grunge and even metal to hardcore.

We also have well-known local musicians who have signed and appeared in our label, such as Marie Emilie Hoch, Love Ness Sitz, Ferdie Knight and Firstblood.

Whenever you listen to our music, play along when you're Pickin' and Rockin'.
