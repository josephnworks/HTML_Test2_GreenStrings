Audio Engineer

  • Music Production / Engineering
  • Selwyn CBD, Selwyn
  • Full Time
  • Closes 5 August 2025

Description:

We are searching for a reliable, detail-orientated audio engineer to join our growing team. The audio engineer’s responsibilities include creating and editing recordings, setting up equipment in studio and at events, following client specifications, and meeting project deadlines. You should have excellent time management and show up on schedule for studio sessions and live gigs.

To be successful as an audio engineer, you should have a discerning ear and be able to identify what sounds good. Outstanding candidates are knowledgeable about audio equipment, able to follow written and verbal instructions, and consistently deliver superb results.


Responsibilities:

  • Understanding client briefs and selecting the appropriate sound and mixing equipment for the project.
  • Communicating with clients and co-ordinating with other staff members to ensure deadlines are met and projects are completed to specifications.
  • Editing, mixing and manipulating recorded and live sound.
  • Setting up equipment safely and on time.
  • Desinging audio systems and advising clients on equipment purchases.
  • Testing and troubleshooting equipment regularly and replacing any faulty components.
  • Fixing any enchancing sound at live events.
  • Collaborating and networking with other Audio Engineers and professionals in the field.
  • Participating in training opportunities and constantly finding new ways to improve skills.

Requirements:

  • High School diploma.
  • Certificate in audio technology or similar recommended.
  • Practical experience with sound equipment.
  • Superb ear for sound.
  • Ability to work under pressure and stay calm when things go wrong.
  • Excellent customer service and interpersonal skills.
  • Professional self-starter with a growth mindset.

Songwriter

  • Songwriting
  • Selwyn CBD, Selwyn
  • Full Time
  • Closes 11 August 2025

Description:

We are searching for a skilled and tech-savvy songwriter to create songs that will resonate with an audience. The songwriter’s responsibilities include analyzing existing hits and music industry trends, networking with industry professionals, and producing work to client specifications and time frames. You should also be able to use traditional instruments and software to create jingles and backing tracks.

To be successful as a songwriter, you should have a wide vocabulary and the ability to identify the elements that make a song appealing. Outstanding candidates are perceptive wordsmiths with a good ear and excellent knowledge of current affairs.


Responsibilities:

  • Meeting with clients and producing songs that fit their specifications.
  • Conducting research and collaborating with other songwriters, musicians and artists to producing quality lyrics and melodies.
  • Generating original ideas and playing around with traditional instruments like pianos, guitars and drums to get a sense of how the piece might sound during a live performance.
  • Using software for editing, mixing and mastering.
  • Discussing songs with clients or musicians and implementing any changes as soon as possible.
  • Creating demos and pitching them to prospective clients.
  • Expanding your skill set by attending music conferences, poetry readings and workshops.
  • Establishing networks and building relationships with industry artists, producers and directors.
  • Building a positive online presence and promoting your work on various platforms.

Requirements:

  • A degree in fine art, music, creative writing or a related discipline might be advantageous.
  • Industry experience and existing connections are recommended.
  • A sound understanding of melody, poetry, music and lyrics.
  • Strong analytical and time management abilities.
  • Good research and marketing skills.
  • Superb written and verbal communication, as well as interpersonal skills.
  • DIligent, deadline-driven and willing to work with other musicians.

Album Cover Designer

  • Graphic Design
  • Selwyn CBD, Selwyn
  • Full Time / Contracted
  • Closes 5 August 2025

Description:

We are seeking a highly creative and detail-oriented Album Cover Designer to conceptualize and design compelling album artwork that captures the essence of an artist’s music and brand. You will collaborate closely with musicians, creative directors, photographers, and marketing teams to produce visually impactful cover designs that resonate with target audiences and align with industry standards.


Responsibilities:

  • Conceptualize and design album covers, back covers, and related promotional materials (e.g., digital thumbnails, vinyl packaging, lyric booklets, posters).
  • Collaborate with artists, music producers, and marketing teams to interpret the vision, mood, and message of the album.
  • Develop original visual concepts that align with the artist's brand and genre.
  • Source or create custom artwork, illustrations, or photography when necessary.
  • Ensure all artwork meets specifications for digital platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.) and physical formats (CD, vinyl, cassette).
  • Maintain awareness of current design trends in music and visual arts.
  • Manage multiple projects and deadlines simultaneously while maintaining high-quality work.
  • Prepare and deliver final assets in appropriate formats for production and distribution.
  • Provide creative input for music video art direction, social media graphics, and other visual assets if required.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as a graphic designer, preferably with a portfolio focused on music or album art.
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) or other professional design tools.
  • Strong understanding of typography, composition, color theory, and visual storytelling.
  • Ability to translate abstract concepts or musical themes into visual form.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
  • Attention to detail and a passion for design innovation.
  • Familiarity with print production processes and digital media specs.
  • Degree or diploma in Graphic Design, Visual Arts, or a related field (or equivalent professional experience).
  • Bonus: Skills in illustration, animation, 3D rendering, or photography.
