About Us

History

Green Strings was founded in 2024 by Devon Righi, who is known to be Selwyn's biggest leading rock singer-songwriter, record producer and former frontman of Deadgone. Since the formation, Righi have signed many local musicians who would lead the rock genre and grown to be one of the most successful record labels in Blossomland, especially being owned independently.

Who is Devon Righi?

Devon Righi is a passionate rock musician and songwriter known for his gritty guitar riffs and raw, emotionally charged lyrics. Born in Selwyn, Blossomland Devon grew up surrounded by the echoes of classic rock vinyls and garage band rehearsals, thanks to his father's love for Led Zeppelin and The Rolling Stones. By the age of 13, he had taught himself to play guitar and began writing original songs inspired by a mix of '70s rock, '90s grunge, and modern alternative influences.

In high school, Devon formed his first band, Deadgone, gaining a local following for their high-energy performances and rebellious sound. Though the group eventually disbanded in 2019, Devon continued to evolve as a solo artist, blending vintage rock tones with contemporary themes. While listening to other growing musicians who inspired by him, he decides to create his own label, to which we know now and the rest is history.

Devon's music explores themes of identity, resilience, and defiance, and his stage presence is marked by a charismatic intensity. Whether playing small clubs or recording in a makeshift studio, Devon remains deeply committed to the soul of rock: authenticity, passion, and a relentless drive to connect through sound.
